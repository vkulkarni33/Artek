Sustainability

Sustainability at Artek Surfin Chemicals

Our Commitment to Eco-Friendly Innovation

At Artek, sustainability isn’t just an initiative—it’s integral to everything we do. We develop cutting-edge surface finishing solutions that not only enhance industrial performance but also prioritize environmental responsibility. From sourcing raw materials to end-use application, we continuously seek safer chemical alternatives and aim to minimize our environmental footprint.

Our operations are guided by globally recognized environmental standards, and we are actively reducing emissions, conserving water, and fostering safer workplaces. By incorporating embedding eco-conscious practices into our research, development, and day-to-day decisions, we’re shaping a cleaner, more responsible future for the industries we serve.

Our Approach to Sustainable Surface Finishing

We are committed to developing advanced surface finishing chemistries that deliver high performance while upholding environmental responsibility. Our solutions are engineered to optimize efficiency, minimize waste, and lower energy consumption throughout the Productsion process.

Each innovation is designed to help manufacturers meet their sustainability goals—without sacrificing operational effectiveness. We work closely with our partners to create custom solutions that address specific application needs while ensuring compliance with environmental standards. From lifecycle assessments to the use of water-based, low-VOC formulations, our approach is rooted in responsibility, driven by innovation, and focused on delivering measurable environmental and performance outcomes.

Sustainability in Action: Ongoing Efforts

At Artek, sustainability is a collaborative journey. We partner closely with our customers to drive continuous improvement through the integration of advanced, environmentally responsible surface finishing technologies. These relationships go beyond Products delivery—they are built on a shared commitment to long-term impact and innovation.

We provide ongoing technical support, hands-on training, and strategic guidance to help clients implement and scale sustainable practices across their operations. From adopting cleaner chemistries to optimizing line efficiency and improving material utilization, we aim to empower our partners to not only meet—but exceed—their environmental goals while enhancing Products performance and aesthetics.

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

At Artek, sustainability is not a one-time achievement—it’s an ongoing commitment. We continuously seek new opportunities to minimize our environmental impact by staying ahead of emerging regulations, Industries trends, and evolving customer expectations. Through regular internal audits, cross-functional reviews, and comprehensive employee training, we ensure that sustainability is embedded in our operations and culture. This proactive approach enables us to refine our processes, develop forward-thinking solutions, and deliver lasting value. Our focusing on continuous, long-term improvement rather than short-term fixes, we help our partners remain future-ready—supporting their environmental goals while contributing to a more sustainable industrial landscape.

Key Sustainability Efforts

01

Eco-Friendly Chemistries

We prioritize the development of surface finishing solutions that incorporate safer, more sustainable chemical alternatives. Our formulations are designed to reduce environmental impact while delivering the high performance our Industries demands. We help customers align with evolving environmental regulations and sustainability goals.

02

Waste Minimization

Efficiency is central to our manufacturing philosophy. We strive to reduce material waste across all stages of Productsion—from formulation to application—by optimizing resource usage and refining our processes. This approach not only supports environmental responsibility but also helps our partners operate more sustainably and cost-effectively.

03

Energy Efficiency

Artek is committed to improving energy efficiency across all manufacturing activities. We routinely evaluate and upgrade our equipment to ensure optimal energy use—contributing to reduced carbon emissions and supporting global climate goals.

04

Environmental Compliance

Our operations are guided by globally recognized environmental standards. Artek’s manufacturing facilities are ISO 14001:2015 certified, affirming our commitment to environmental management systems that actively reduce our ecological footprint and ensure regulatory compliance.

Sustainable Products Innovations

Eco-Friendly Plating Solutions

Artek has developed advanced plating technologies that reduce reliance on hazardous chemicals, offering safer and more sustainable alternatives for industries such as electronics and automotive. These solutions support environmental compliance while maintaining high-performance standards.

Water-Based Formulations

Many of our Productss are engineered as water-based alternatives, significantly lowering emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). This not only contributes to cleaner air but also helps create a safer working environment for users.

Long-Lasting, Durable Coatings

Our coatings are built for durability, designed to extend the service life of industrial components. By reducing the need for frequent reapplication, these solutions help conserve materials, reduce maintenance, and lower the overall environmental impact over time.

Our Sustainability Goals

Reduce Products Environmental Impact

Continue developing and refining surface finishing solutions that minimize ecological harm throughout their lifecycle—from raw materials to end-use.

Enhance Energy Efficiency

Improve energy performance across all Productsion processes by upgrading equipment, optimizing operations, and embracing energy-saving technologies.

Minimize Waste Generation

Reduce waste at every stage of manufacturing while advancing responsible waste management and recycling practices.

Maintain Environmental Compliance

Ensure full compliance with local and global environmental regulations, including ongoing certification to leading standards such as ISO 14001:2015.
