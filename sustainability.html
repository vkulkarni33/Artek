Sustainability

Sustainability at Artek Surfin Chemicals

A Commitment to Eco-Friendly Solutions

At Artek, we believe in creating innovative surface finishing solutions that not only enhance the performance of industrial products but also contribute to environmental protection. Sustainability is embedded in our core operations, driving us to minimize our environmental impact while delivering high-quality products and services. We are committed to aligning our practices with global environmental standards and continually working towards reducing our ecological footprint.

Our Approach to Sustainable Surface Finishing

We are dedicated to developing surface finishing chemistries that provide both effective results and environmental responsibility. Our products are designed to optimize performance, minimize waste, and reduce energy consumption during production. Artek is committed to offering solutions that help industries meet sustainability goals without compromising quality or performance.

Key Sustainability Efforts

01

Eco-Friendly Chemistries

Our products are designed with sustainability in mind, using safer chemical alternatives where possible to reduce the environmental impact of surface finishing processes. We focus on developing solutions that meet industry needs while ensuring minimal environmental harm.

02

Waste Minimization

We aim to optimize the use of materials and reduce waste during manufacturing. By focusing on efficiency, we strive to minimize the environmental impact associated with the production and use of our products.

03

Energy Efficiency

Artek focuses on improving energy efficiency throughout our manufacturing processes. We regularly review and upgrade our equipment to ensure we are using energy efficiently, contributing to a reduction in carbon emissions and overall energy consumption.

04

Environmental Compliance

Artek’s operations and products comply with leading global environmental standards. Our manufacturing facilities are certified under ISO 14001:2015, ensuring we adhere to environmental management practices that help reduce our overall environmental footprint.

Sustainable Product Innovations

Eco-Friendly Plating Solutions

Artek has developed surface finishing solutions that aim to reduce the use of certain chemicals, promoting safer and more sustainable alternatives for industries such as electronics and automotive.

Water-Based Solutions

Many of our products are formulated to be water-based, helping to reduce emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and ensuring a safer working environment for our customers.

Durable Coatings

Our coatings are designed for durability, aiming to extend the lifespan of industrial products and reduce the need for frequent re-coating, which in turn conserves resources over time.

Sustainability in Action: Ongoing Efforts

Artek works closely with our customers to continuously improve their sustainability efforts through the adoption of advanced surface finishing solutions. Our goal is to help customers meet their environmental targets while enhancing product performance.

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

Artek is committed to continuously improving its sustainability practices, actively seeking out new ways to reduce our impact on the environment. By focusing on long-term sustainability, we aim to support our clients and partners in achieving their own environmental goals. Contact us to learn more about how we can help your business adopt more sustainable surface finishing practices.

Our Sustainability Goals

Reduce the environmental impact of our products

Increase energy efficiency in our production processes

Minimize waste generation and improve waste management practices
