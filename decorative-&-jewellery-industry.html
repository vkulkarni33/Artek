Decorative & Jewellery Industries

ARTEK supports the decorative and jewellery Industries with specialized plating solutions that deliver brilliant finishes, high ductility, and excellent wear resistance. Our range of brighteners and nickel-free alternatives ensure safety and aesthetics for high-end applications.

Our Solutions

1. Nickel-Free Plating
  1. Duralloy YBM: Yellow bronze with gold-like finish, ideal for jewellery.
  2. Duroalloy 6820 WB: White bronze, palladium substitute for costume jewellery.
2. Bright Nickel & Chrome Plating
  1. MAGNUM 897 / HALLMARK AS Series: High gloss nickel deposits for elegant finishes.
  2. TRISTAR / SKYLARK TCM: Trivalent chrome and tin-cobalt for mirror-like decorative appearance.
3. Gold & Silver Plating
  1. AUROMAX NUTRA: 22–24 carat decorative gold for flash coatings.
  2. ARGOASTRA / ARGOMAX: Bright silver for jewellery, cutlery, spectacle frames.
4. Electrophoretic Coatings & Sealants
  1. DUROCLAD 4700 M / TEKNOCLEAR EPA 2020: Transparent coatings for anti-tarnish and UV resistance.

Why Choose ARTEK for Decorative & Jewellery?

  1. Non-toxic, safe-to-use decorative finishes.
  2. Rich, uniform, and durable coatings for luxury applications.
  3. Long-lasting protection against tarnish and wear.

Applications

Rings, Bangles, Earrings
Buttons, Zippers, Spectacle Frames
Costume Jewellery
Decorative Cutlery & Gift Items
