About Us

Welcome to Artek Surfin Chemicals

Artek Surfin Chemicals Ltd. is a trusted name in the surface finishing Industries, delivering advanced chemical technologies for over 30 years. Founded in 1989, we specialize in high-performance solutions for:

  • General Metal Finishing
  • Anodising & Electrocoloring
  • Precious Metal Platings
  • Multi-layer PCB Platings
  • Palting Plastics
  • Electroclouring Lacquer
  • Zinc Alumax Flake coating

Headquartered in Mumbai, India, Artek operates three world-class manufacturing units in Vasai, Daman, and a cutting-edge facility in Tarapur. We’ve expanded globally with the acquisition of two PVC additive plants in the USA and Germany, now operating as Galata Chemicals LLC and Galata Chemicals GmbH.

Our ISO-certified facilities (ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018) uphold the highest standards in quality, environmental sustainability, and occupational health and safety. Today, Artek boasts:

  • A turnover of over ₹3000 crores
  • A passionate team of 300+ professionals
  • A commitment to excellence in chemistry, innovation, and customer support

Join us as we continue to shape the future of surface finishing with science, service, and sustainability at the core of everything we do.
